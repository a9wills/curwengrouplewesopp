01 — Executive Summary

A flexible campus opportunity on a gateway A24 site in Worthing.

Broadwater Road Campus: Parcel 1 is presented as a premier educational and commercial opportunity in Worthing, available on either a freehold or leasehold basis with all enquiries invited.

The site occupies a gateway position on Broadwater Road, the A24 main route into Worthing from the north, and is positioned as Phase 1 of a major mixed-use regeneration masterplan including retail, healthcare and senior living.

The opportunity can accommodate requirements up to approximately 70,000 sq ft GIA, with occupiers able to select the amount of space they require rather than choosing between fixed size options.
