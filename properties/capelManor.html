01 — Opportunity for SEN School Providers

A proposed specialist SEN school within a secure, self-contained campus.

Situated within the grounds of Capel Manor College’s Enfield Campus, this proposal provides an opportunity to establish a purpose-built specialist SEN school within a secure and self-contained setting whilst benefiting from access to a broad range of existing educational facilities.

A key benefit of the proposal is the opportunity for pupils attending the SEN school to engage with the Further Education college as they approach college age. Pupils will be able to participate in supervised “taster days” and introductory vocational sessions, allowing them to explore a range of practical learning pathways and courses available at the college. This will help support smoother educational transition routes and improve long-term outcomes by enabling students to experience vocational subjects in a familiar and supportive environment.

02 — Executive Summary

An exceptional educational environment.

Curwen Group, working alongside Capel Manor College, is seeking expressions of interest from specialist SEN school operators for the delivery of a new purpose-built SEN school within the college’s Enfield Campus.

The proposed development comprises

New school building: 12,000 sq ft two-storey SEN school building.

Secure site: 1.83-acre site.

Access: Dedicated internal access road.

Parking: 40-space car park.

External sport: Multi-Use Games Area (MUGA).

Outdoor provision: Sensory and therapeutic outdoor space.

Heritage: Retained historic gardener’s cottage and gardens.

Estate link: Direct connections to the wider Capel Manor estate.

What makes this different

What differentiates this opportunity from a conventional school development is the relationship with Capel Manor College and the established facilities already available across the wider campus.

The proposed school will benefit from a safe and secure standalone environment whilst being situated within an established educational estate.

04 — Strong Demand for New SEN Provision

Growing need across Enfield and North London.

The requirement for additional specialist school places continues to increase across London and the Home Counties.

Rising EHCP Demand

Enfield Council has identified increasing demand for specialist educational provision as the number of children with Education Health and Care Plans continues to rise.

Local Specialist Placement Pressure

Like many local authorities, Enfield faces ongoing pressure to secure sufficient local specialist placements and reduce reliance on expensive out-of-borough provision.

National SEND Context

Nationally, the number of EHCPs has increased significantly in recent years, creating substantial demand for new specialist settings.

Operator Opportunity

For operators, the scheme offers the ability to establish a new specialist school in a location where demand is expected to remain strong for many years.

Purpose-built response to need

The proposed development provides an opportunity to create high-quality local provision within a purpose-built setting capable of meeting this growing need.
