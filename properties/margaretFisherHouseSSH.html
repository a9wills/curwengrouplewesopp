01 - Executive Summary

A proposed 9-apartment Specialist Supported Housing scheme.

Margaret Fisher House is proposed to provide 9 self-contained one-bedroom apartments for adults with learning difficulties within a Specialist Supported Housing (SSH) setting.

Each apartment will provide a minimum of approximately 45 sq.m of accommodation and the property will be fully refurbished to meet modern SSH compliance requirements, accessibility standards and supported living expectations.

The current proposal converts the property from 12 units to 9 larger one-bedroom flats, with completed units expected to be deliverable in late 2026 / early 2027, subject to design development, statutory requirements, procurement and construction programme.
